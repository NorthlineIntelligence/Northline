## 3. Maturity Tier Model

| Tier | Operational Posture | Score Range |
|------|--------------------|------------|
| Fragmented | Reactive | 1.0 – 2.3 |
| Emerging | Structured | 2.3 – 3.1 |
| Operationalizing | Systemic | 3.1 – 4.1 |
| Institutionalized | Strategic | 4.1 – 5.0 |

Tier reflects transformation maturity — not technical experimentation level.

---

## 4. Weighted Tier Calculation

Tier is calculated using weighted pillar averages.

Weighting:
- Strategic Coherence → 30%
- Human Alignment → 30%
- System Integrity → 25%
- Sustainability Practice → 15%

Transformation succeeds through strategy and people.
Technology reinforces but does not lead transformation.

---

## 5. Structural Risk Flags

Tier alone is insufficient.

### Critical Weakness Flag
Triggered if any pillar < 2.0

### Structural Imbalance Flag
Triggered if variance between highest and lowest pillar exceeds threshold.

### Adoption Risk Flag
Triggered if:
Human Alignment < 2.5
AND
System Integrity > 3.5

This pattern indicates technocratic fragility.
